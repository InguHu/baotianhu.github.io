BIOGRAPHY

Baotian Hu is now a third-year PhD students of ICRC at Harbin Institute of Technology, Shenzhen Graduate School, China. His advisor is Prof. Qingcai Chen. His current research interests lie in the areas of deep learning, natural language processing and handwritten Chinese recognition, specifically for automatic summarization, question answering and sentence matching. He received his M.E degree in Department of Computer Science and Technology at the Harbin Institute of Technology Shenzhen Graduate school (HITSZ) in 2012. Before that, he received the B. E. degree in Department of Information and Computing Science from Shandong University of Science and Technology, Qingdao, China, in 2010.
